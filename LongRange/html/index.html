Planning Tools

Tools and viewers for planning in the Tahoe Basin

Public web apps for exploring zoning, deed restrictions, and project-specific spatial analyses across the Lake Tahoe region.

Zoning & Land Use

Parcel-level lookup
What can I do on my parcel?
Enter an address or APN to see the zoning district, tolerance district, special designations, and the full list of permitted uses for any parcel in the basin.

Use-driven search
Where can I build X?
Pick a use category, then a specific use type, to see every zoning district in the basin where that use is permitted or conditionally approved, with density limits and plan details.
